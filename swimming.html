Swimming Sport

During the summer eve of last year,we held a swimming competition at Grand-Regency Hotel in Nairobi. The activity was sponsored by the Master-Card- Foundation that cheer to improve the social awareness of the girl child. The theme mainly focuses on the issues affecting the girl child and measures towards improving them.The following therefore are events that took place. Pads distrubution
Swimming costumes distribution
Food and Drinks session
Open talks
