Horse-Riding

Horse-riding activity involves competition where one is required to have the required horse-riding skills so as to participate.
The annual context always take place between June and August every year.
The activity can be conducted for fun or as part of the gamble however,
it depends on the agreement of the participant.
The following are best places to hold Horse-ridinga activities.
Bantu-lodge in Laikipia
Ngong Forest in Nairobi
Savannah resort in Naivasha
The great Resort in Maasai Mara park
